Cost

One financial hurdle self-driving cars must overcome before they are available to consumers everywhere is cost. LIDAR systems, a critical component in accurate autonomous systems cost upwards of 100k.Their cost can be attributed to the sophistication of the system as well as not being in high demand. Meaning early adopters will bear a large burden of the price.

Technology

Self-driving cars still experience issues when recognizing objects which were segmented by other objects. Self-driving cars struggle to navigate in urban environments due to their inability to recognize segmented objects.

Bureaucracy

Differences on the state level on what constitutes a street legal autonomous vehicle it isn't immediately clear to manufactures if their car will be road legal in every state. While in the past road readiness was transitive for vehicles from state to state this isn't the case with self-driving cars.For auto manufacturers to be encouraged to take part in the autonomous revolution changes must be made to legal guidelines.
